Shinebit Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2025

1. Data Collection and Use

Shinebit does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All user records are saved locally on your device and are not shared with any third parties.

2. App Functionality

The app does not require login or internet access to function. Your gratitude records and calendar data remain private and under your control.

3. Data Sharing

Shinebit does not share your data with any third parties. Your data is stored locally on your device and is only accessible by you.

4. Children's Privacy

Shinebit is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

5. Security

Shinebit takes reasonable measures to protect the data stored on your device. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Shinebit may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated policy with a revised "Last updated" date. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
